How We Got Started

Hi! I’m Bob, a husband, dad, US Navy veteran, computer guru,
and if it breaks I can fix it!
I have been in the computer/printer repair business
since 1990, and electronics since my Navy days. Several
years ago, I started thinking about the future, and
retirement from my career. I have serviced many kinds
of computers, printers, electronics in general, built
things, remodeled houses and RVs, rebuilt automobile
engines, and thought maybe it might be fun to try
something new. Printers have ink, sewing machines have
needles so they’re cousins, right? You’d be surprised
how comparable they are!

Since my wife of 48 years has been a sewing maniac most
of that time, making most of our clothes, costumes,
formals, quilts, even upholstering furniture, she has
accumulated several high-end machines. I tinkered with
them here and there. Though they didn’t stump me, I
thought it might be a good idea to have some formal
training so I wasn’t missing anything important.

As the story goes, I attended the Sewing Machine
Institute in Texas, and became officially certified
to repair all kinds of sewing machines. As a plus, we
even had a course in vacuum cleaners! I’ve been at it
for a few years, but it’s time to officially get down
to business!

I look forward to serving you, keeping your machines up
and running…”keeping you in stitches”!
